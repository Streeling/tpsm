Rezolvări¶

14.361. Dacă \(Y=\frac{X-m}{\sigma}\), atunci \(Y\) are distribuția \(N(0,1)\). Într-adevăr, \(M(Y)=M(\frac{X-m}{\sigma})=\frac{M(X)-m}{\sigma}=0\) și \(D(Y)=D(\frac{X-m}{\sigma})=\frac{D(X)}{\sigma^2}=1\). Funcția de repartiție a variabilei aleatoare \(Y\) se notează prin \(\Phi\).

Vom încerca să exprimăm funcția de repariție \(F_X\) a variabilei aleatoare \(X\) prin \(\Phi\) în felul următor:

\[\begin{split}F_X(x)=P\{X<x\}=P\{\sigma\cdot Y+m<x\}=P\{Y<\frac{x-m}{\sigma}\}=\Phi(\frac{x-m}{\sigma}).\end{split}\]

14.362. \(p_k=P\{|X-m_X|<k\sigma\}=2\Phi(k)-1.\) Respectiv:

  1. \(p_1=2\Phi(1)-1\approx 2\cdot 0,8413-1\approx 0,6826\).
  2. \(p_2=2\Phi(1)-1\approx 2\cdot 0,9772-1\approx 0,9544\).
  3. \(p_3=2\Phi(1)-1\approx 2\cdot 0,9987-1\approx 0,9974\).

14.363.

\(P\{|X-10|<\varepsilon\}=2\Phi(\frac{\varepsilon}{5})-1\Rightarrow \Phi(\frac{\varepsilon}{5})=\frac{P+1}{2}\).

  1. \(\Phi(\frac{\varepsilon}{5})=\frac{0,9974+1}{2}=0,9987\Rightarrow \frac{\varepsilon}{5}\approx 3\Rightarrow \varepsilon\approx 15\Rightarrow X\in (-5,25)\).
  2. \(\Phi(\frac{\varepsilon}{5})=\frac{0,9544+1}{2}=0,9772\Rightarrow \frac{\varepsilon}{5}\approx 2\Rightarrow \varepsilon\approx 10 \Rightarrow X\in (0,20)\).
  3. \(\Phi(\frac{\varepsilon}{5})=\frac{0,5+1}{2}=0,75\Rightarrow \frac{\varepsilon}{5}\approx 0,67\Rightarrow \varepsilon\approx 3,35 \Rightarrow X\in (6,65;13,35)\).

14.364. \(P\{|X-1,84|<0,02\}=2\Phi(\frac{0,02}{\sigma})-1=0,999\Rightarrow \Phi(\frac{0,02}{\sigma})=\frac{0,999+1}{2}=0,9995\Rightarrow \frac{0,02}{\sigma}\approx 3,3\Rightarrow \sigma\approx 0,006\).

Rezultă că \(P\{|X-1,84|<0,01\}=2\Phi(\frac{0,01}{0,006})-1\approx 2\Phi(1,67)-1\approx 2\cdot 0,9525 -1\approx 0,905\).

14.365. \(P\{X<12\}=0,15\Rightarrow \Phi(\frac{12-m}{\sigma})=0,15\Rightarrow \Phi(-\frac{12-m}{\sigma})=1-\Phi(\frac{12-m}{\sigma})=1-0,15\Rightarrow \Phi(-\frac{12-m}{\sigma})=0,85\Rightarrow -\frac{12-m}{\sigma}\approx 1,04\Rightarrow m-12\approx 1,04\sigma\).

\(P\{X>16,2\}=0,4\Rightarrow P\{X<16,2\}=1-0,4\Rightarrow \Phi(\frac{16,2-m}{\sigma})=0,6\Rightarrow \frac{16,2-m}{\sigma}\approx 0,25\Rightarrow 16,2-m\approx 0,25\sigma\).

14.366. \(P\{|X-m_X|<10\}=2\Phi(\frac{10}{5})-1=2\Phi(2)-1\approx 2\cdot 0,9772-1\approx 0.9544\approx 95%\).

14.367. \(P\{|X-m_X|<10\}=0,98\Rightarrow \Phi(\frac{10}{\sigma})=\frac{0,98+1}{2}=0,99\Rightarrow \frac{10}{\sigma}\approx 2,33\Rightarrow \sigma\approx 4.292\).

14.368. \(P\{X<1\}=0,05\Rightarrow \Phi(\frac{1-1,06}{\sigma})=0,05\).

Echivalent \(\Phi(-\frac{1-1,06}{\sigma})=1-\Phi(\frac{1-1,06}{\sigma})=1-0,05=0.95\Rightarrow -\frac{1-1,06}{\sigma}\approx 1.65\Rightarrow \sigma\approx 0,03636\).

Respectiv \(P\{X>0,940\}=1-P\{X<0,940\}=1-\Phi(\frac{0,94-1,06}{0,03636})\approx 1-\Phi(-3,3)=\Phi(3,3)\approx 0,9995\).

14.369. \(P\{|X-20|<\varepsilon\}\approx 2\Phi(\frac{\varepsilon}{0,4472})-1=0,95\Rightarrow \frac{\varepsilon}{0,4472}=0.975\Rightarrow \frac{\varepsilon}{0,4472}\approx 1,96\).

Astfel \(\varepsilon\approx 0,8765\).

14.370. \(P\{d_1\leq X< d_2\}=\Phi(\frac{d_2-\frac{d_1+d_2}{2}}{\alpha(d_2-d_1)})-\Phi(\frac{d_1-\frac{d_1+d_2}{2}}{\alpha(d_2-d_1)})\).

14.373. \(x_{n1}=m-\sigma\), \(x_{n2}=m+\sigma\). \(P\{X\geq x_{n2}\}=1-P\{X< x_{n2}\}=1-\Phi(\frac{x_{n2}-m}{\sigma})=1-\Phi(\frac{m+\sigma-m}{\sigma})=1-\Phi(1)=1-0,8413=0,1587\). \(P\{x_{n1}\leq X\leq x_{n2}\}=\Phi(\frac{m+\sigma-m}{\sigma})-\Phi(\frac{m-\sigma-m}{\sigma})=\Phi(1)-\Phi(-1)=\Phi(1)-(1-\Phi(1))=2\Phi(1)-1=2\cdot 0,8413-1=0,6826\).

14.374**. \(Q(\sigma)=\Phi(\frac{b-m}{\sigma})-\Phi(\frac{a-m}{\sigma})\); \(Q'(\sigma)=-\frac{1}{\sigma^2}((b-m)\Phi'(\frac{b-m}{\sigma})-(a-m)\Phi'(\frac{a-m}{\sigma}))\). Respectiv \(Q'(\sigma)=0\Leftrightarrow (b-m) e^{-\frac{(b-m)^2}{2\sigma^2}}-(a-m)e^{-\frac{(a-m)^2}{2\sigma^2}}=0\)

14.375. \(M(|X-m_X|)=\int_{-\infty}^{\infty}|x-m|f(x)dx=\int_{-\infty}^m(x-m)f(x)dx-\int_m^{\infty}(x-m)|f(x)dx\)

14.376. \(a_X=\frac{\mu_3}{\sigma_X^3}=\frac{0}{1^3}=0\).

\(e_X=\frac{\mu_4}{\sigma_X^4}-3\), \(\mu_4=\mu_2+2=(2+1)\sigma^2\cdot\mu_2=3\cdot 1^2\cdot 1^2=3\), deci \(e_X=\frac{3}{1^4}-3=0\).
